History

The Colts have competed as a member club of the NFL since their founding in Baltimore in 1953. They were one of three NFL teams to join the AFL to form the AFC following the 1970 merger.

Lucas Oil Stadium

After 24 years of playing at the RCA Dome, the Colts moved to their new home Lucas Oil STadium in the fall of 2008. Lucas Oil Products won the naming rights to the stadium for 20 years.
